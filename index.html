Who I Am

My name is Stéphane WETINHOUN. I received a B.Sc. in Electrical and Computer Engineering from the Higher National Institute of Industrial Technology (former IUT) of Lokossa, Benin Republic, in 2019. In October 2022, I graduated for a research M.Sc. In ICT (with focus in AI-Data Science) from the Institute of Mathematical Sciences and Physics (IMSP) in Benin.
Prior to my MSc classes, I did two internships in software programming in Benin Republic. I also worked for a local company specializing in Web App development.
Currently, I'm providing Software Engineering services at SLI AFRIKA. Here, I'm collaborating with a team to develop Web APIs using NodeJS. I'm also diving into Data Analytics

Away from academics, I’m also a voluntary member of the “Benin Chapter of Internet Society”. Recently, I completed a six(6) months remote internship in Artificial Intelligence (AI) at IBM Research Laboratory Africa - Nairobi (Kenya). During this internship, I discovered the concept of Trustworthy AI. Most expecially, I exployed anormally detection in datasets for Fairness in AI.

My research interests include and are not limited to: Internet of Things (IoT) applications, Networks and Systems, Software Engineering, Machine Learning and Data Science.
